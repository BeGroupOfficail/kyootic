About Us

Delivering Innovative Finishing & Supply Solutions for Every Project

Founded in Egypt in 2025, KYOOTIC provides innovative finishing solutions and comprehensive supply services for construction, infrastructure, and electromechanical sectors. We help contractors source all project materials from one reliable partner.

We provide MEP, infrastructure, pipes, valves, pumps, cables, and finishing materials for all project needs, ensuring high-quality products with efficient logistics and on-time delivery for every project scale.

As a trusted distributor and supply partner in Egypt, KYOOTIC works with leading manufacturers and agents to support contractors and developers with reliable sourcing, strong partnerships, and integrated supply solutions that simplify project execution.

Our Massion

Delivering Reliable Supply Solutions for Construction Excellence

At KYOOTIC, we strive to meet our clients' needs by providing a diverse range of products and services tailored to construction, infrastructure, and electromechanical projects.
