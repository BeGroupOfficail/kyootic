Pipes & Fittings

KYOOTIC provides comprehensive supply solutions for contracting companies, handling all procurement needs from a single, reliable source. We are a trusted distributor of major brands for the following categories:

1. UPVC Pipes & Fittings

UPVC pipes are used in many applications, including:

  • Water supply and sewage networks
  • Irrigation networks

UPVC pipes come in many different sizes and diameters, and many approved brands are available.

2. PPR Pipes & Fittings

PPR pipes are used in water supply networks.

  • Hot and cold water distribution
  • High pressure networks

PPR pipes come in many different diameters and sizes, and many approved brands are available.

Download PDF

3. HDPE Pipes & Fittings

HDPE pipes are used in many applications, including:

  • Water supply and sewage networks
  • Irrigation networks
  • Firefighting networks

HDPE pipes come in many different sizes and diameters, and many approved brands are available.

Download PDF

4. Seamless Black Steel Pipes & Fittings

Seamless black steel pipes are used in various applications, but they are most commonly and widely used in firefighting.

  • Fire protection systems
  • High temperature and pressure lines

Seamless black steel pipes come in many different sizes and diameters, and most seamless pipe charts are available. Many certified brands are also available.
